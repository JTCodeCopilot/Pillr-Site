Apple Health vs Pillr
for medication reminders

Apple Health is useful for basic medication tracking on iPhone. Pillr is a better fit when you want a tool built around ADHD medication routines, fast logging, and clearer follow-through.

Updated April 13, 2026

When Apple Health is enough

Apple Health can be enough if you mainly want a simple place to track medications and get a basic reminder on iPhone.

When Pillr is a better fit

Pillr is stronger when the hard part is not setting a reminder. It is staying consistent, logging quickly, checking what happened later, and keeping your routine from falling apart on messy days.

  • One-tap dose logging
  • Focus windows for stimulant timing
  • Refill tracking
  • Clearer history and exports
  • A calmer flow designed around ADHD medication

The simplest way to think about it

Choose Apple Health if you want basic tracking inside an Apple app you already have.

Choose Pillr if you want a dedicated app that makes reminders easier to act on and easier to trust afterwards.

Final thoughts

Both can help. Pillr is built for the people who need more support than a simple reminder can give.

Pillr is better for

  • ADHD medication routines
  • Fast dose logging
  • Clearer history
  • Refill planning
  • Focus timing
