Adderall guide

Forgot to take Adderall?
What to do next

If you forgot to take Adderall, what to do next depends on the time, your own prescription instructions, and advice from your clinician or pharmacist.

Updated April 13, 2026

General information only Use your own prescription instructions first. If you are unsure, ask your pharmacist or clinician.

Start with timing

The main question is how late you are. Taking a dose much later than usual may affect your day or make sleep harder, depending on the medication type and your own routine.

Do not double up unless you were told to

If you missed a dose, do not take extra later unless your clinician has told you to do that.

Why it happens

Missed doses usually come from disrupted routines, busy mornings, travel, or not being sure whether you already took it.

How to make it less likely next time

  • Use a dedicated reminder app
  • Log the dose the moment you take it
  • Keep the medication in one regular place
  • Check refill timing before you run low
