Vyvanse guide

Forget to take Vyvanse?
What to do next

Forgetting to take Vyvanse can happen, especially on busy mornings, weekends, or days when your routine changes.

What you should do next depends on the time of day, your prescription instructions, and advice from your doctor or pharmacist. This page is a general guide only and should not replace medical advice for your situation.

Back to home

First, check how late it is

Vyvanse is a long acting medication, so taking it too late in the day may affect sleep.

If you realise you missed a dose, the safest next step is usually to check:

  • How many hours late you are
  • Whether you were given instructions for missed doses
  • Whether taking it later could interfere with sleep or appetite

If you are unsure, contact your pharmacist or prescribing clinician.

Do not double up unless you were told to

If you forgot a dose, do not take extra later to make up for it unless your clinician has specifically told you to do that.

Taking more than prescribed can increase the risk of unwanted effects.

Why missed doses happen so often

Missed ADHD medication is usually not a motivation problem. It is often a routine problem.

Common reasons include:

  • Waking up late
  • A disrupted morning routine
  • Travelling
  • Changing work schedules
  • Not being sure whether the dose was already taken
  • Relying on memory alone

For many adults with ADHD, the real challenge is not understanding the routine. It is remembering it consistently.

How to make missed doses less likely

A few small systems can make a big difference:

  • Use a dedicated medication reminder app
  • Keep medication in a consistent place
  • Link the dose to the same part of your morning
  • Log each dose as soon as you take it
  • Use refill planning so gaps do not catch you out

When to ask a professional

Speak with your doctor or pharmacist if:

  • You often miss doses
  • You are unsure whether to take a late dose
  • Your medication schedule no longer fits your routine
  • You want a safer missed dose plan for future situations

Final thoughts

If you forgot to take Vyvanse, do not panic. Check the time, avoid doubling up unless you were told to, and get medical advice when unsure.

Then focus on making tomorrow easier with a system you can trust.
