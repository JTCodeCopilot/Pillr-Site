About

Why Pillr exists

Pillr exists because medication routines are often harder to manage than they should be, especially for people with ADHD. The goal is not to make health feel more clinical. It is to make daily medication easier to remember, easier to trust, and less mentally heavy.

What Pillr is trying to solve

For many people, the hardest part is not knowing they should take medication. It is remembering in the moment, knowing whether they already did, and having a record they can trust later.

Who Pillr is for

Pillr is designed for people who want a calmer way to manage ADHD medication on iPhone. It can also be useful for everyday medications, but the strongest focus is helping with ADHD routine friction.

What matters most

  • Clear reminders
  • Fast dose logging
  • A reliable history
  • Better routine stability
  • Less second-guessing

What Pillr is not

Pillr is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace professional advice.

In one sentence

Pillr is an iPhone app that helps adults with ADHD remember medication, log doses, and keep a clear record without adding more mental clutter.
