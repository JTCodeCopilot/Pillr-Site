What an ADHD medication tracker should do

A useful tracker should help you:

  • Log taken doses
  • Record missed doses
  • Check your medication history
  • Keep notes when needed
  • Stay aware of refill timing

Tracking is not about making things complicated. It is about reducing uncertainty.

Why tracking helps

Many people with ADHD know the feeling of trying to remember:

  • Did I take it this morning?
  • Did I skip yesterday?
  • Am I nearly out?
  • Have I been consistent this week?

Without a record, it is easy to guess. A tracker replaces guessing with something you can check in seconds.

Why iPhone users often want a dedicated app

You can use Notes, Reminders, or a spreadsheet, but those tools were not built specifically for medication routines.

A dedicated iPhone medication tracker makes the process faster and easier by keeping reminders, logs, and history in one place. If reminders are your main concern, start with this medication reminder app guide.
