About this FAQ

This page holds the full question list, while the homepage stays more compact and easier to move through.

Does Pillr replace medical advice or a clinician?

No. Pillr helps you track and organise medications, but it does not replace a clinician.

Can I track non-ADHD medications in Pillr?

Yes. Pillr supports any medications, with special timing tools for ADHD stimulants.

Where is my data stored?

Your data stays on your device by default.

Can I export my medication history?

You can export medication history as CSV or PDF for easy sharing with clinicians or caregivers.

Is Pillr a medication management app?

Pillr is built for medication management. You can set reminders, log doses, and review adherence on your device so your routine stays steady.

Does Pillr include an ADHD symptom tracker?

Pillr keeps symptom context in mind by capturing focus windows, side effect notes, and wear-off check-ins alongside medication data.

Can I use Pillr as a pill tracker with reminders?

Yes. Pillr combines pill tracker features with flexible reminders so you always know when to take, skip, or snooze a dose.

Why is Pillr called an ADHD app?

Pillr was designed around the timing, consistency, and routine challenges that often come with ADHD medication management.

How does Pillr support medication adherence?

Pillr tracks adherence rates, streaks, and refill status, making it easier to stay consistent and share a clear history when needed.

Can Pillr track stimulant medication schedules?

Pillr supports custom medication names and timing details, so you can track stimulant schedules such as Adderall, Vyvanse, or methylphenidate as directed by your clinician.

Is Pillr an iPhone app for ADHD medication?

Yes. Pillr is an iPhone app designed for ADHD medication reminders, dose tracking, focus window logging, and adherence history exports.
