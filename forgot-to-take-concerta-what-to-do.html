Concerta guide

Forgot to take Concerta?
What to do next

If you forgot to take Concerta, the safest next step depends on your timing, your own prescription instructions, and advice from your clinician or pharmacist.

Updated April 13, 2026

General information only Use your own prescription instructions first. If you are unsure, ask your pharmacist or clinician.

Start with timing

Concerta is a long-acting medication, so timing matters. The later you notice the missed dose, the more important it is to be careful.

Do not double up unless you were told to

Do not take extra to catch up unless your clinician has clearly told you to do that.

Why missed doses happen

Routine changes, rushed mornings, weekends, travel, and simple uncertainty are common reasons people miss a dose.

How to make it less likely next time

  • Use a reminder that fits your real routine
  • Log the dose the moment it happens
  • Keep a clear history you can check later
  • Stay ahead of refills
